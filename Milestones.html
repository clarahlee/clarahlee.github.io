Typical Sound Development by Ages

  • /h/ as in “help”
  • /m/ as in “more”
  • /n/ as in “no”
  • /p/ as “pot”
  • /b/ as in “bear”
  • /d/ as “dog”
    By age 3:
  • Zh as in “treasure”
  • Y as in “yes”
  • /w/ as in “we”
  • Ng as in “sing”
  • /t/ as in “top”
  • /k/ as in “car”
  • /g/ as in “go”
    By age 3 yrs and 6 mo.:
  • /f/ as in “feet”

  • Sh as in “shoe”

    By age 8yrs and 6 mo.:
  • Th as in “thing”
